Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Bald Research operates the RE5 brand and the website at re5-website.vercel.app (hereafter "the site"). This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Email addresses — when you subscribe to receive the 8 Drivers Checklist or any other lead magnet, your email address is collected and stored in our email service provider (Kit / ConvertKit).

Purchase information — when you buy a digital product through the site, your transaction is processed by Payhip and either Stripe or PayPal. We receive notification of the purchase including your email address. We do not store payment card details — these are handled by the payment processors.

Browser information — basic anonymized analytics may be collected (page views, country, referrer) to help us understand which content is useful. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

2. How We Use Information

We use your email address to deliver the lead magnet you subscribed to, to send the welcome email sequence about the RE5 framework, and occasionally to share educational content or product updates. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email.

Purchase information is used to deliver your purchased products and to provide customer support if you contact us about an order.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties.

We share information only with the service providers necessary to operate the site:
— Kit (ConvertKit) for email delivery
— Payhip for digital product fulfillment
— Stripe and PayPal for payment processing
— Vercel for hosting

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs how they handle your data.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:
— Access the information we hold about you
— Correct inaccurate information
— Request deletion of your information
— Unsubscribe from email at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email [your-contact-email-here].

5. Data Retention

We retain email subscriber information until you unsubscribe and request deletion. Purchase records are retained for tax and legal compliance reasons (typically 7 years in most jurisdictions).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Significant changes will be communicated via email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, email [your-contact-email-here].
